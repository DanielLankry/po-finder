# Po — Simple Ads Plan

> **Budget**: 300 NIS/month (~$80)
> **Approach**: Phase 1 = static ads on Meta. Phase 2 = Instagram influencers.
> **Goal**: Get paying businesses ($5/month) + grow consumer side.

---

## The Whole Plan in One Picture

```
Month 1-2  →  Static ads on Instagram + Facebook  (test & learn)
Month 3-4  →  Add 2-3 micro-influencers on Instagram
Month 5+   →  Double down on whichever works best
```

That's it. Don't overthink.

---

## Phase 1 — Static Ads (Months 1-2)

### What you run
**One Meta campaign. Two ad sets. Three static images each.**

| Ad Set | Audience | Daily Budget | Goal |
|--------|----------|--------------|------|
| Business Owners | Israel, 25-55, "small business / עצמאי / בעל עסק" interests | 7 NIS | Get businesses to sign up ($5/mo) |
| Consumers | Tel Aviv metro, 22-45, "food / cafés / local" interests | 3 NIS | Get app/site usage |

**Total: 10 NIS/day = 300 NIS/month**

### The 3 static ads (per audience)

**For business owners:**
1. **Pain hook** — "אתה עסק קטן בלי נראות בגוגל? תופיע על המפה של פה." + map screenshot
2. **Offer** — "תופיע על המפה של ישראל ב-$5 לחודש. ללא התחייבות." + simple price card
3. **Social proof** — Photo of a real listed business with quote/testimonial

**For consumers:**
1. **Discovery** — "גלה עסקים קטנים ליד הבית" + map screenshot with pins
2. **Events** — "מה קורה השבוע באזור שלך?" + event card visual
3. **Favorites** — "שמור את המקומות האהובים עליך" + favorites screen

### How to make the images (free)
- Use **Canva** (free) — Instagram Post template (1080x1080)
- Take screenshots of your own app
- Add text in Hebrew, big and clear, one message per image
- Don't hire a designer — your own screenshots ARE the creative

### What to do every week
- **Monday**: Check the dashboard. Note which ad has the most clicks/cheapest.
- **Friday**: Kill the worst-performing ad. Replace it with a new variation.
- That's the whole optimization process.

### When to move to Phase 2
Move when **any one** of these is true:
- You have 10+ paying businesses (proof the funnel works)
- You've run static ads for 8 weeks and CPA is stable
- You spot a small Instagram creator who already posts about local cafés/food

## Phase 2 — Instagram Influencers (Months 3+)

### Who to hire (NOT big influencers)
**Micro-influencers only**: 5,000 — 30,000 followers. Local Israeli creators who post about food/cafés/markets/local stuff.

### How to find them
1. Search Instagram hashtags: `#תלאביב #אוכלאמיתי #קפה #שוקהפשפשים #בתימדינה`
2. Look for accounts with **high comment-to-follower ratio** (engagement, not size)
3. DM them yourself — no agency needed

### What to pay
| Follower range | Pay per post | Pay in product |
|----------------|--------------|----------------|
| 5K-10K | 100-200 NIS OR free 6-month listing | Best for early |
| 10K-30K | 300-500 NIS OR partnership deal | When you have budget |

**With a 300 NIS/month budget**: do 1 micro-influencer per month, or trade free listings.

### What you ask them to post
Pick **one** of these — never all three:
- **Story takeover**: They walk through 3 small businesses in their city using Po to find them
- **Reel**: "I found this hidden café using Po — here's why" (30-second authentic review)
- **Carousel**: 5-pin map showing their favorite local spots, all listed on Po

### Budget split when influencers start
| Item | NIS/month |
|------|-----------|
| Static ads (keep running winners) | 200 |
| 1 micro-influencer | 100 |
| **Total** | **300** |

## Tracking — Just 2 Things

You don't need fancy tracking yet. Just install:

1. **Meta Pixel** on your site (one snippet, takes 10 minutes)
2. **A UTM link** for each influencer (`?utm_source=ig&utm_campaign=creator_name`)

Check Meta Ads Manager weekly. Check Vercel Analytics (you have it) for influencer traffic spikes.

## Simple Success Targets

| Metric | Month 2 | Month 4 | Month 6 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Paying businesses | 5 | 15 | 30 |
| Cost per business signup | < 60 NIS | < 50 NIS | < 40 NIS |
| Influencers active | 0 | 1-2 | 2-3 |

If you're not hitting Month 2 numbers → **don't add influencers yet**. Fix the static ads first.

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't run 5 ad sets** — start with 2. More splits the budget too thin.
2. **Don't hire a big influencer** — 100K followers ≠ 100K relevant Israelis. Micro wins.
3. **Don't change ads daily** — let each ad run 5-7 days minimum before judging.
4. **Don't pay influencers in cash if you can offer free listings** — your product IS the currency.
5. **Don't chase consumer growth before businesses pay** — businesses are the revenue. Consumers come from organic + influencers.
