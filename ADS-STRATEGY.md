# ADS-STRATEGY.md — פה (Po) Strategic Advertising Plan

> **Date**: March 2026 (Updated)
> **Budget**: 300 NIS/month (~$80 USD)
> **Business**: Two-sided marketplace — small business discovery platform (Israel)
> **Revenue**: $5/month per business subscription
> **Goal**: Acquire paying businesses + drive consumer traffic

---

## Executive Summary

With a 300 NIS/month budget, traditional SaaS or marketplace ad strategies (which typically require $1,500–$5,000+/month) are not viable. Instead, this plan focuses on **surgical micro-targeting** using Meta (Facebook/Instagram) as the sole paid platform, supplemented by free/organic channels that act as force multipliers.

**Core insight**: Po is a two-sided marketplace. You need businesses (supply) AND consumers (demand). At this budget, **prioritize business acquisition** (direct revenue) and use organic/viral tactics for consumer growth.

**Break-even math**:
- 1 business subscription = $5/month = ~18 NIS/month
- Budget: 300 NIS/month
- Need 17 paying businesses just to cover ad spend
- Target CPA for business subscriber: < 50 NIS (to be profitable within 3 months of LTV)

### What's New (March 2026 Update)

Since the initial strategy, Po has added significant new features that strengthen both the value proposition and ad messaging:

| New Feature | Ad Impact |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Events system** | Businesses can create & promote events → new ad angle ("discover local events") |
| **Favorites/wishlists** | Consumer engagement loop → deeper funnel, retargeting signal |
| **Weekly schedule** | More value for business owners → stronger feature-list ads |
| **SEO (sitemap, robots, OG images)** | Organic search growth → reduces dependency on paid |
| **Enhanced UI** (animations, shimmer buttons, marquee) | Higher-quality landing experience → better CVR |
| **Business selector** | Multi-business dashboard → upsell potential for owners with multiple locations |
| **OpenGraph images** | Social sharing generates free impressions |

## Three-Track Strategy

### Track A: Business Acquisition (65% of budget = 195 NIS/month)

**Objective**: Get small business owners to subscribe at $5/month

**Why this gets 65%**: This is your revenue. Every paying business funds future growth. A falafel stand owner paying $5/month for 12 months = $60 LTV. At 50 NIS CPA, that's 3.3x ROAS.

**Updated value prop** (leverage new features):
- Appear on the map + full business profile
- Create and promote events (markets, specials, openings)
- Manage weekly schedule (not just daily hours)
- Upload photos, receive reviews
- Multi-business support (own multiple listings)

### Track B: Consumer Awareness (25% of budget = 75 NIS/month)

**Objective**: Drive app/site usage so businesses see value in being listed

**Why this matters**: Businesses won't pay if no one is using the map. Consumer traction is social proof for business acquisition.

**Updated angles** (leverage new features):
- "Discover local events near you" (Events feature)
- Save your favorite spots (Favorites feature)
- Real-time open/closed status (weekly schedules)

### Track C: Events Promotion (10% of budget = 30 NIS/month)

**Objective**: Drive traffic to specific business events on the platform

**Why this is new**: The Events feature creates a time-sensitive, shareable content type that generates urgency and virality. Even at 1 NIS/day, event-based ads create a "what's happening near me" discovery loop.

**Strategy**: Boost 1-2 high-quality business events per month when listed businesses create events. If no events exist yet, reallocate this 10% to Track A.

## Target Audiences

### Business Owners (Track A)
- **Age**: 25-55
- **Location**: All Israel (start with Tel Aviv + Central District for density)
- **Interests**: Small business, entrepreneurship, food trucks, street food, bakery, coffee shop
- **Behaviors**: Small business owners, page admins, Facebook shop owners
- **Job titles** (if available): Owner, בעל עסק, עצמאי
- **Lookalike**: Once you have 50+ subscribers, create 1% lookalike from customer list

### Consumers (Track B)
- **Age**: 18-45
- **Location**: Start with Tel Aviv metro (highest density of small businesses)
- **Interests**: Street food, local food, coffee, foodie culture, supporting local businesses
- **Behaviors**: Frequently travels, uses food delivery apps
- **Custom**: Retarget website visitors (install Meta Pixel immediately)
- **New signal**: Users who added favorites but haven't visited in 7+ days → retarget

### Event Attendees (Track C)
- **Age**: 18-45
- **Location**: 10km radius around event location
- **Interests**: Same as Track B + events, nightlife, weekend activities, markets
- **Timing**: Run 3-5 days before event date, stop day after

## Tracking Setup (Must Do Before Launching Ads)

### Meta Pixel — CRITICAL

Install the Meta Pixel on po.co.il before spending a single shekel. Without it, Meta can't optimize for conversions and you're burning money.

**Events to track**:
| Event | Trigger | Purpose |
|-------|---------|---------|
| `PageView` | Every page load | Base tracking |
| `Lead` | Business clicks "התחילו עכשיו" on pricing page | Track business interest |
| `Purchase` | Stripe checkout completion (callback page) | Track paying subscribers |
| `ViewContent` | Business profile page viewed | Track consumer engagement |
| `Search` | Map search/filter used | Track consumer engagement |
| `AddToWishlist` | User adds business to favorites | **NEW** — Track consumer intent |
| `Schedule` | User views event details | **NEW** — Track event engagement |

### UTM Parameters
All ad links must include UTMs:
```
?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign={campaign_name}&utm_content={ad_name}
```

### Google Analytics 4 (Free)
Install GA4 to track site traffic, user behavior, and conversions independently from Meta.

## Free/Organic Channels (Force Multipliers)

With 300 NIS/month, organic growth is not optional — it's essential. These cost only time:

### 1. Facebook Groups (Highest ROI)
- Join Israeli small business owner groups (e.g., "עסקים קטנים בישראל", "עצמאים")
- Post value-first content: "בנינו מפה חינמית שעוזרת ללקוחות למצוא עסקים קטנים — מוזמנים לבדוק"
- Do NOT spam. Provide value. Answer questions. Build trust.
- **Expected impact**: 5-10 business signups/month from groups alone

### 2. Instagram Reels (Consumer Growth)
- Post 3x/week: 15-30s Reels showing businesses on the map
- **NEW**: Feature business events in Reels ("מה קורה בשכונה השבוע?")
- Content: "גילינו את [business type] הכי טוב ב[city]" format
- Use location tags, food hashtags, local hashtags
- **Expected impact**: 500-2,000 organic reach per Reel

### 3. SEO (NEW — Now Active)
- Sitemap and robots.txt are now live → Google can index all business pages
- OpenGraph images generate rich social previews when shared
- Each business profile is a unique indexed page → long-tail search traffic
- **Expected impact**: 50-200 organic visits/month by Month 3, growing over time

### 4. Google Business Profile (Free)
- Create a GBP for "פה" as a tech/service company
- Helps with local SEO for "עסקים קטנים ליד" type searches

### 5. WhatsApp Business (Direct Outreach)
- Create a WhatsApp Business account
- Personally reach out to small business owners you find (markets, food trucks)
- Send: "שלום! אני מפה — פלטפורמה שעוזרת לעסקים קטנים להיראות. רוצה לנסות חינם?"
- **Expected impact**: Highest conversion rate of any channel (personal touch)

### 6. Partnerships
- Approach municipal small business associations
- Offer bulk rates or co-marketing
- Contact food truck event organizers
- **NEW**: Partner with event venues/market organizers to cross-promote on Po's Events feature

### 7. Social Sharing (NEW — Passive Growth)
- OpenGraph images mean every shared Po link looks professional on social media
- Encourage businesses to share their Po profile link → free impressions
- Business events are inherently shareable → organic amplification

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Budget too small to exit Meta learning phase | Ads don't optimize | Run only 1 ad set per track, keep audience broad |
| Low business supply → consumers see empty map | Consumer churn | Seed map with free listings in Tel Aviv first |
| Creative fatigue (same ads to same audience) | Rising CPC, falling CTR | Refresh creatives every 2-3 weeks |
| Businesses churn after 1 month | Negative ROI | Events + schedule features increase stickiness |
| No conversion tracking | Can't optimize | Install Pixel BEFORE spending anything |
| Events feature underused | Track C budget wasted | Reallocate to Track A until events are active |
